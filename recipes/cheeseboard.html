The Ultimate Cheese Board

Having friends over? Want to dazzle them with your wit, charm, and class? Prepare the ultimate cheese board!

Ingredients

You guessed it--Cheese! But let's get more detailed than that

  • Brillat Savarin (triple cream brie)
  • Aged Gouda (preferably 2-yr)
  • Sharp White Cheddar
  • Gorgonzola (there should be a blue representative!)
  • Pink Lady Apple
  • Fresh Strawberries
  • Castelvetrano Olives
  • Almonds
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Cut the Cheese(s)!
    • You'll want to make sure each type of cheese has its own shape. Try triangle wedges for the brie, artful rocks for the gouda, elongated rectangles for the cheddar, and crumbles for the blue.
  2. Slice apples & strawberries
  3. Assemble it all on a wooden board--be sure to put the honey in a vessel!
