Falafel Recipe

Make homemade falafels with the best of them! For this recipe, you will need dried chickpeas, fresh mint, fresh parsley, fresh cilantro, and oil for frying

To make sure you have something to go with your homemade falafels, have pita on hand, plain greek yogurt, cucumber. Pickled red onions also make a fine addition!

The Falafel Balls - Ingredients

  • 8oz dried chickpeas
  • 1 package fresh mint
  • 1 bundle fresh parsley
  • 1 bundle fresh cilantro
  • 4 cups vegetable oil for frying

Toppings

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Chopped Cucumber
  • Pickled Onion
  • Hummus

Steps

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight
  2. Throw soaked chick-peas and herbs into a food processor
  3. Blend until finely chopped
  4. Form mixture into 2-inch balls
  5. Heat oil on stove until simmering
  6. Add the falafel balls and fry until golden brown
  7. Remove from pan with tongs onto a plate with a paper-towel to soak up excess oil
  8. Eat plain or make a sandwich using pita and the recommended toppings!
